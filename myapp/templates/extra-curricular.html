Extra-Curricular Activities

We encourage holistic development through diverse student activities and clubs.

National Service Scheme (NSS)

Students participate in social activities like cleanliness drives, blood donation camps, and community services under NSS.

Sports & Athletics

Regular inter-college and inter-departmental competitions are organized in cricket, kabaddi, volleyball, chess, etc.

Cultural Activities

Annual cultural fest includes dance, singing, drama, rangoli, debates, and celebrations of national events.

Student Clubs

Literary club, nature club, and entrepreneurship cell are student-led groups for skills beyond academics.

Yoga & Wellness

Regular yoga and wellness workshops are held to promote physical and mental well-being among students.

Eco-Club & Clean Campus

The college runs various campaigns for a clean and green campus, plastic-free zone, and tree plantation.
