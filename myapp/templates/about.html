Establishment

{{school_name}} was established in the year 1959, {{school_name}} is affiliated to Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University, Bhagalpur. BadriNarayan Mukteshwar College, Barhiya is the oldest institute of higher learning in the District Lakhisarai.

Late Badri Narayan Mukteshwar has a dream to open a higher learning institute for the students of rural area. B.N.M College, Barhiya believe in providing quality education and information about courses to students & their parents to matching their needs in collaboration of Search Education Portal.

The College ensures publicity and transparency in the admission process. It is very particular to cater to the needs of differently abled students. The college is primitives to gender sensitivity, inclusive education and environment friendly ethos among students.

The Purpose of the {{school_name}}, is to stimulate and coordinate the institutional resources of the College and to challenge faculties and students.

The chief objective of the college since its inception has been to shape the young minds with the urge for creativity, spirit of tolerance and scientific temper. To cope with changing need of the society and economy.

Affiliation

Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University, Bhagalpur has 42 affiliated Colleges. In the beginning, college was affiliated to Bihar University and later to Tilka Manjhi Bhagalpur University. At the beginning time college had only Arts Faculty and in 1968, college started science faculty with Biology.

Late Gopeshwar Pd Singh was the first secretary of B.N.M College and Late Mahesh Pd Singh, Ex. Agriculture Minister Bihar Govt. was first chairperson of the college. Late Lala Babu and a few other social workers were in the governing body of the college. After crossing several hurdles, they achieved the goal and made their dream come true by establishing a college in rural area. In 1962, college was shifted to its own building. New building was situated on the bank of Ganga River. Now B.N.M. College Barhiya is running in 4 Acre compound with 2500 SqMtr Built Area.

The college ensures publicity and transparency in the admission process. It is very particular to cater to the needs of differently abled students. The college is promotive to gender sensitivity, inclusive education and environment friendly ethos among students.
