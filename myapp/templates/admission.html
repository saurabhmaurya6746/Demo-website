ADMISSION PROCEDURE

{{ school_name }} provides education from Nursery to Class 12 for both boys and girls, offering a strong foundation in academic and co-curricular development. All parents and guardians seeking admission for their children are advised to go through the School Prospectus carefully, which contains important information regarding eligibility, fee structure, and curriculum details.

Admissions will be granted strictly on the basis of merit and availability of seats, following the guidelines set by the school management and educational authorities. Once the seats are filled, no further admissions will be entertained.

ADMISSION PROCEDURE
  • Admission requests (for Class 11 Arts & Science) will not be accepted without a properly filled admission form.
  • No personal communication will be made for admission updates. All notices will be displayed on the school notice board.
  • Eligible students can collect the admission form from the school office during working hours.
  • All important announcements will be put up on the School Notice Board and Website.
  • Selected students must attend the admission counselling on the mentioned date, failing which the seat may be allotted to others.
  • Seats for OBC, EBC, SC & ST categories are reserved as per Government norms. Valid caste certificates must be submitted.
  • Students must select their subject combinations carefully during admission. No changes will be allowed later.
  • The original and one set of attested photocopies of the following documents must be submitted at the time of admission:
  1. Migration certificate from the institution last attended.
  2. Mark sheet of the examination passed.
